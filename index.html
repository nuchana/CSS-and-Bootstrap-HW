About Me

I have been a marketer creating consumer brands and goods for over 8 years and now I am looking for a new adventure of a career shift. Coding has become my main interest because I am excited by new innovations and applications created by coders. These offer a real solution to consumers and users. That has inspired me to be a part of this career journey. I enjoy creating tangible products in my current line of work and shifting to coding will be exciting and challenging as I will create and build intangible work that can be used in everyday life through digital coding.

I am originally from Bangkok, Thailand and I have been moving around USA, Spain, Thailand, and Singapore in the last 10 years. Partly, it was for my study and work that I had to move and travel. I love travelling, meeting new people and experiencing new cultures. Tennis and scuba diving are my go-to hobbies when I have free time; however, moving to USA in the east coast is not easy to find a place for scuba diving. The water temperature can be freezing even in the summer month!

My journey in digital coding has just begun and can't wait to learn more about building my new skills in the next 6 months. I am a full-time employee and taking a new program can be daunting and definitely out of my comfort zone but if I do not take my chance now, I will regret for not pursuing it for sure.
